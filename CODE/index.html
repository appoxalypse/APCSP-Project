한국드라마 (Korean Drama)


Korean dramas have been around since 1962. The dramas' origins were in Korean radio dramas during Japanese rule in Korea. As television sets became more commonplace, Korean dramas grew in popularity. The dramas started in historical settings but evolved into romance later. With the increase in technological resources, kdramas have branched into many genres, such as action, horror, sci-fi, and more.
