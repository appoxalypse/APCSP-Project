Web Novels


Webnovels noramlly refer to novels or stories that are online. The web novels that this website refers to are Asian translated novels that are published online. Websites such as Novel Updates keep track of new chapter releases. This website serves as a catalogue for Asian novels that are being or have been translated. These webnovels are generally thousands of chapters long and relate to some form of spiritual cultivation.
